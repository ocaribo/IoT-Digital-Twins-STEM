Chemistry Scenario – Reaction Monitoring
🎯 Learning Objective(Endothermic and Exothermic Reactions)

Students will understand how chemical reactions can be monitored through measurable parameters.

🧪 Activity

Students observe a simple controlled reaction and identify changes in measurable variables.

 Use of System

Sensor data is collected during the reaction and displayed in the digital twin interface, showing real-time variation of parameters.

📊 Expected Outcome

Students gain understanding of how abstract chemical changes can be represented digitally.
